Welcome to the CA-935th Kitty Hawk Air Society! Kitty Hawk is our AFJROTC unit’s national honor society. We are dedicated to helping every single cadet in the unit with all of their academic needs. By providing daily walk-in and weekly tutoring on all weekdays, morning, lunch, and afterschool, we hope to help you as tutors, study buddies, and mentors! We also have events such as study sessions, workshops, lectures, and much more. Our goal is to create new and healthy perspectives on academics, help high schoolers balance their lives as students and cadets, and assist in developing helpful and productive study techniques. The Commander of the Kitty Hawk Air Society is c/2d Lt Annalisa Boquiren, the AOIC is c/2d Lt Bowie Murthy, and the NCO is c/TSgt Akshar Udas. Reach out to us on Discord (CC - @annalisa_251, AOIC - @lilithswoe, NCO - @axr.28) if you have any questions!
